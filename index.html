San Pedro Cafe

Exceptional island tastes in Hudson, Wisconsin

San Pedro Café is a Caribbean themed restaurant and bar sharing the flavors, culture and pleasures of the Caribbean Sea with residents and visitors of Hudson, Wisconsin. Nestled along the historic main street district of Hudson, San Pedro Café is located in original home of The First National Bank built in the 1870's.

  • Caribbean fare
  • wood-fired pizza
  • rum and tequila
  • historic venue
