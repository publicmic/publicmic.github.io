San Pedro Cafe

Exceptional island tastes

San Pedro Café is a Caribbean themed restaurant and bar sharing the flavors, culture and pleasures of the Caribbean Sea with residents and visitors of Hudson, Wisconsin. The cafe is nestled along Hudson's historic main street district. San Pedro Café has preserved many elements of the original home of The First National Bank built in the 1870's.

  • Caribbean fare
  • wood-fired pizza
  • rum and tequila
  • historic venue
